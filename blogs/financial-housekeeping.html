Financial Housekeeping

Many of our prospective new clients are in their early to mid-50s. This tends to be when we take stock and look to the future. Until then, it’s a case of ‘head down and go,’ building a career and raising a family. But now the children are growing up, life is changing, and there’s a realisation that we have a later life that might need to be planned for.

One of the issues can be the amount of financial baggage we accumulate along the way. You’ve probably had various jobs with pension schemes that you left behind. Some of them might not even write to you anymore because you’ve moved house a couple of times. Different investments and assets can appear over time. Statements arrive at different times of the year that are all worded differently and full of jargon. Who knows whether they are still the right investments for you?

It can all feel a bit random and disorganised. That’s not a criticism; it’s just life getting in the way. We’re all very busy so there’s definitely an admin benefit in having someone look at this for you.

It used to be the case that to achieve a spread of investments, you needed to scatter your money between various companies. That’s no longer the case, and it’s possible to place the money with one company and gain access to pretty much any investment that’s on the market.

We see the relief on our clients’ faces when they can see where their money is, and everything is neat and tidy!

That said, it might be that there’s a valid reason why something should be left where it is. Certain pensions and investments have valuable guarantees built in that would be lost if you transferred away. This is why advice is so important. Even if you are left with investments with various providers, we’ll have a record of this and will keep an eye on everything for you. In our regular planning meetings, we’ll remind you of everything you have and how it all fits into your financial plan.

What is the money for? How much do you need to be financially independent from work at some stage? What’s the right investment strategy to give you a financial plan where you can do all the things you want to do and have the right amount of money available at the right time?

It’s a lot to ask for you to do this on your own, but we can help.
